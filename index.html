10 Years Experience

Setheo Engineering started operating in 2007. The first offices were based in South Africa with a small group of 12 employees.The company has since expanded and now has over 500 employees, with offices in Congo Brazzaville, Congo Kinshasa, Uganda, Nigeria, U.S.A, Mozambique, Ivory Coast, Zambia, Ghana and Zimbabwe. Our key areas of service include; electrical, civil, mechanical, building and telecoms engineering. Our clientele consists of municipalities, governments, and private entities.
