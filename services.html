    Our Services

Services Engineering Unit

We are a full-service engineering, construction, environmental and consulting solutions firm. Our personnel include engineers, architects, construction experts, planners, estimators, economists, technicians and scientists, representing virtually all construction design disciplines. We plan, design, permit, construct and manage facilities, with one mission in mind: Producing the best services for our clients.

Electrical

Setheo Engineering is recognised as one of the largest high voltage substation construction company in Africa, with a capacity to construct substations from 11kV to 765kV. Our specialised engineers design, procure, construct and commission conventional outdoor substations (AIS), indoor cellular substations, gas-insulated substations (GIS) and 22kV-132kV mobile substation skids for open-cast mining.

General Building

Building activities cover the full scope of traditional construction providing infrastructure including healthcare facilities, retail and parkade developments, residential, office accommodation, educational institutions, hotels & leisure and industrial facilities. We also undertake select residential developments for major mining and industrial clients, low cost and affordable housing for the public sector as well as prefabricated or modular construction for select clients.

Civil

The specialised business unit incorporates design, modern engineering practices and strong project management to undertake various infrastructure development projects. The Civils & Roads Division together with their Traffic, Transportation, Geotechnical and Bridges Divisions, can offer their clients an integrated one-stop service covering the complete "Life Cycle" of Road and Transportation Engineering.

Mechanical

The specialised business unit incorporates design, modern engineering practices and strong project management to undertake various mechanical engineering projects.
