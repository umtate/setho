Safety, Health & Environmental Policy

As a company that is driven by a pursuit for sustainability, Safety, Health and Environment (S.H.E) comes first in everything we do and is a core value for Setheo Engineering. We foster this by comprehensive risk and environmental assessment for every project and appointing experienced teams who combine a deep understanding of the management of Safety, Health and Environment with that of our clients.

  • Developing and implementing S.H.E policies, systems, procedures and standards in line with nationally and internationally recognized practices and standards.
  • Providing adequate and appropriate financial, material and human resources to implement the S.H.E Policy and our S.H.E system.
  • Complying with all applicable national laws and regulations and where appropriate, institute additional measures to ensure the highest safety, health and environmental standards and operational practices.
  • Identifying and assessing safety, health and environmental hazards and aspects in all our activities, and continuously reduce the risks and impacts to generally acceptable levels.
  • Setting annual safety, health and environmental objectives and targets for ourselves.
